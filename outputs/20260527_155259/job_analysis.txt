Here is a thorough analysis of the Data Analyst job description:

**Job Analysis: Data Analyst, Department of Health and Human Services**

**1. Required Technical Skills:**
*   Python
*   SQL
*   Tableau
*   Data Visualization

**2. Soft Skills and Qualities:**
*   Communication (specifically report writing for senior stakeholders)
*   Collaboration (working with engineering teams)
*   Analytical Thinking (ability to analyze health data)

**3. Experience Level and Years Required:**
*   Minimum 2 years of experience

**4. Key Responsibilities:**
*   Analyze health data using Python and SQL.
*   Build dashboards in Tableau.
*   Write reports for senior stakeholders.
*   Collaborate with engineering teams.

**5. Top 5 Resume Keywords:**
1.  Python
2.  SQL
3.  Tableau
4.  Data Analysis / Data Analyst
5.  Data Visualization

**6. What the Agency Seems to Value Most:**
The Department of Health and Human Services appears to value a data analyst who possesses a strong foundation in core technical data skills (Python, SQL, Tableau) and can immediately apply these skills to analyze health-related data. Furthermore, they highly value the ability to translate complex data insights into clear, actionable reports for senior leadership and to work effectively in a collaborative, cross-functional environment. Practical experience (2 years) with these tools and responsibilities is also a key priority.
